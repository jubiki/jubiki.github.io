Websites


Yes, we can do that for you.

If we made your site, please drop us an email with your name and change request. Once we know what we're dealing with, we'll send you a payment link. Most changes are $10 each. We charge $20 for more complicated changes.

If your site was made by someone else, we'll need to see their code before making any changes. Drop us an email with your FTP details. If you don't have that information, drop us an email with your hosting company, login information and web address. Once we see what we're working with, we'll send you a payment link. Most changes are $20, but complicated changes are $30.



We keep a backup of websites that were made by us. However, our backups are of the original version of your site. The only sites with updated versions are those with a maintenance plan.

To request a backup, drop us an email with your web address, name and request. We'll send you a copy in a zip file.
