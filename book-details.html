General Information

We always conduct research before diving into the design process. First, we must understand your audience and read a sample of your manuscript ourselves. Second, we research popular trends within your genre. You will not recieve a book cover in a few days.

We'll need to know what size your printed book will be.

Turn around for book covers can vary. Some styles are quicker to complete than others. Your completed package will include a PDF file, image file, and editable AI or PSD file.

We do not print books or provide you with an ISBN. We design book covers only.

Full Book Cover (Front, Back and Spine)
