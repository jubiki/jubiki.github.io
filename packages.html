Branding Guide

A branding guide is a resource that provides consistency to your personal or compay brand on online and in print. It's a way to tell people how you want to be represented to the public. Your branding guide will include the following:

  • About
  • Mission or Focus Causes (If applicable)
  • Brand Voice
  • Moodboard
  • Color Palette
    • Primary Palette
    • Secondary Palette
    • Palette Usage
  • Typography
  • Logo Variation and Usage
