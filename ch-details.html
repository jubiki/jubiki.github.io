General Information

You are reponsible for providing the images for your prints. If you're ordering comp cards, you must provide a minimum of five images including a headshot. We help you choose images best suited to print. All images must be 300DPI for opitmal printing. These images must be recent.

Comp cards are printed on the standard 5.5 x 8.5 card stock. Headshots are printed on the 8 x 10 standard headshot paper.

The current turnaround for comp cards and headshots is 2 weeks.

Comp Cards

When you order a comp card, you'll be asked to provide information like hair color, eye color, height, shoe size and your measurments. (ex: dress size, hips, bust, shirt, suit, inseam and sleeve).

There are several layouts a comp card can be printed in:

Layout 1 (Front Only)—Headhsot has full white border around your headshot with your name centered at the bottom.

Layout 2 (Front Only)—Headshot has full bleed (no borders) with your name centered on the bottom.

Layout 3 (Front Only)—Headshot has full bleed (no borders), except a white border at the bottom where your name will be printed.

Layout 4 (Back Only)—Three image layout with white or black borders, information on the bottom.

Layout 5 (Back Only)—Four image layout with or without borders (borders can be black or white), information on the bottom.

Headshots

We have several layouts that headshots are printed in:

Layout 1—A full white border around your headshot with your name centered at the bottom. This is a perfered layout of casting dirctors, as they can make notes in the white space.

Layout 2—A full bleed printing that leaves no border around your headshot. Your name is not seen anywhere on the print. This is a popular print for autographs.
