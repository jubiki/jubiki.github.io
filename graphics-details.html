Social Media

Orders for custom social media graphics is done in a set of three platforms for one order. The number of graphics you receive depends on the platforms you've chosen. Here is a breakdown of the different platforms:

Platform # of Graphics
Facebook 1 Graphics - Banner
Twitter 1 Graphics - Banner
Youtube 3 Graphics - Profile Photo, Banner and Watermark
Etsy 2 Graphics - Shop Icon and Banner
Linkedin 1 Graphics - Banner

We won't design custom thumbnails for your Youtube videos. We stay away from custom profile photos, unless you're a business.

Logo Design

We'll help you choose a logo style ideal for you. After we've determined a style, we'll design two initial logo concepts for you to choose from. You'll be allowed two revisions. When completed, you'll recieve:

  • Logo for web and print -
    • Scaleable vector files (.ai and .esp files)
    • A vector PDF file
    • One high resolution logo on a white background in CMYK format for priting
    • One high resolution logo on a white background in RGB for internet usage
  • Final logo design in 3 different versions -
    • Full-color logo
    • Grayscale logo
    • Logo file for a colored background
  • Small version logo for favicon or icon.
