No, you don't have to pay upfront.

If you're ordering comp cards, headshots, thank you cards, you'll receive a single payment link after approving your proofs.

For other services, you'll receive two payment links:

  • First payment is expected after your order is accepted
  • Second payment is expected after you've approved your proofs

The total cost of your product is divided into two charges.

If you're ordering a package or a website, you'll receive three payment links:

  • First payment is expected after we've accepted your order.
  • Second payment is expected after approving your designs.
  • Final payment is expected prior to delivery.

The total cost of your product is divided into three charges.

We Review Orders

We review every order submitted to us for possible issues. Should there be an issue with your order, we'll contact you to request further information or to recommend changes. This helps us avoid future problems with your projects.

The more information you give us on your forms, the easier and quicker your project will progress. If you need help, please feel free to contact us.
